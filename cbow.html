    Continuous bag of words

    Cbow

    The input to the model could be wi−2,wi−1,wi+1,wi+2wi−2,wi−1,wi+1,wi+2 , the preceding and following words of the current word we are at. The output of the neural network will be wi. Hence you can think of the task as "predicting the word given its context" Note that the number of words we use depends on your setting for the window size1

    Compared with Skip-gram, Cbow several times faster to train than skip-gram and slight better accuracy on frequent word. Skip-gram have better performance than Cbow on small corpus.
